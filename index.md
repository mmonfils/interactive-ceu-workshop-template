# Interactive CEU Workshop Template Hub

Welcome to the web-facing portal for the **90-Minute Interleaved CEU Presentation Template**. This open-source repository serves as an architectural blueprint for educators looking to build low-tech, mobile-optimized, high-engagement presentations.

## 🛠️ Available Structural Templates
* **Curriculum Design Canvas:** Read our [Core Blueprint and Pacing Guide](README.md) to review the 15-minute interleaved structural pacing.
* **Continuing Education Compliance:** View the plain-text [CEU Evaluation Submission Form](EVALUATION.md) layout.
* **Academic Reuse Guidelines:** Check out our open-source [Contribution & Forking Instructions](CONTRIBUTING.md) to clone this framework for your own presentation.
